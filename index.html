Inheritance Excerise - Card Game

When studying object-oriented programming in one of my classes, I wrote a collection of card games in c++ for users to play. Group up with a team of people and verse each other in blackjack, slap jack, or guess the card! Navigate through menus and play a textbase game. You can view the leaderboard by viewing all displaying the players, remove players when they start crushing you, add and remove games to the queue to play, and lastly but most importantly play the games! Be wary of the segfaults you might encounter and good luck playing the games because I never added instructions.
