Projects

Card Games

When studying object-oriented programming in one of my classes, I wrote a collection of card games in c++ for users to play. Group up with a team of people and verse each other in blackjack, slap jack, or guess the card! Navigate through .menus and play a textbase game. You can view the leaderboard by viewing all displaying the players, remove players when they start crushing you, add and remove games to the queue to play, and lastly but most importantly play the games! Be wary of the segfaults you might encounter and good luck playing the games because I never added instructions.

Steam Game Finder

A group and I got together for the WiCS hackathon and created a discord bot in a weekend! We combined the Steam API and Discord API to peak at your Steam library and share the games you and your friends have in common to find games to play together. In order to use the bot, you'll have to register and give your Steam username and you'll be on your way!

Robotics

In high school, I joined my high school's robotics team that was a part of the FIRST Tech Challenge. Every year, we designed a robot to compete in the current year's challenge programming an autonomous and driver mode and creating mechanical components to launch wiffle balls, for example, or sensors to read color of a beacon. I worked on the programming team and worked on the autonomous mode of the robot so the robot could perform tasks by itself. One of the programs that I worked on navigated to a white line on the field, followed the line, and sensed the color of the beacon and pushed the corresponding color for the team we were on.
